Basketball Scratch Game

Built in MIT Scratch while teaching a young student of mine who was really driven to become a developer. A simple idea that turned into a full game — rebounds, shooting mechanics, and all.

basketball-game.sb3 · MIT Scratch
How to play
Movement
WMove forward
AMove left
SMove backward
DMove right
Shooting
SpaceShoot
Aim the target at the centre of the rim. Once it lines up, hit space to shoot. Rebounds are thrown back to you — move into position and pick the ball back up.
