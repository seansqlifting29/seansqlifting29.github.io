About me

Engineer by training.
Developer by choice.

I started as a geotechnical engineer at Georgia Tech — where I learned to approach complex systems analytically, think in structures, and solve problems from first principles. That mindset never left. It just found a new home in code.

Today I build clean, purposeful front-end experiences using React Native, TypeScript, and Framer. I care deeply about how things work under the hood, how they feel to use, and how they perform at scale. Whether it's a notification system, a design-to-code workflow, or a full Framer site with CMS and automation — I build it to last.

I've shipped production features at Padel App, contributed to open-source data tooling at Just Train, and competed in two hackathons — winning one. Right now I'm looking for the right team to build something worth seeing.
